I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Rutgers University, advised by Prof. Dimitris Metaxas. My research centers on agentic AI, multimodal memory, and retrieval-augmented reasoning systems.

I am actively open to research collaborations and full-time opportunities — please feel free to reach out.
